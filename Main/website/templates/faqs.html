Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section for the PoseParfaite Web App. Here you'll find answers to common questions about our app.

1. What is PoseParfaite?

PoseParfait is a web app designed to help you improve your posture and fitness through guided exercises and personalized feedback.

2. How does the app track my pose?

The app uses your device's camera to analyze your movements and provide real-time feedback on your posture and exercise form.

3. Can I create multiple profiles?

Yes, you can create multple profiles.

4.Does the app store my camera data?

No, the app processes your camera data locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your video feed.
